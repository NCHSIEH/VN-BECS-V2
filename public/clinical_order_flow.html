Clinical Order Flowchart

VN-BECS V2.0 System Integration

Document: Order Requisition

Status: Approved

Ref: SOP 07 / SOP 10 / AABB

Step Subsystem / Feature Workflow & Requisition Logic
01 Transfusion Service (Hospital)
Clinical Ordering & Emergency Requisition
Physicians place ordering requisitions for specific patients in the system. Clinicians can request standard crossmatching or trigger a 1-click STAT "Massive Transfusion Protocol" (MTP) for trauma emergencies.
02 Immunohematology & Reference Lab (IRL)
Order Interoperability & Queue Alarm
The clinical request instantly pushes to the IRL Control Tower. Standard requests enter "Pending Crossmatch" (待處理交叉匹配). Emergency MTP orders trigger flash-red alarms in "Urgent Clinical Requests" (緊急臨床血袋申請) for prioritized processing.
03 IRL Compatibility Testing Node
Compatibility Testing & Match Verification
The medical technologist fetches the patient's MRN and antibody history. The system enforces strict matching protocols: Electronic Crossmatch (EXM) for antibody-free patients, and Serological IS/AHG for patients with positive antibody history.
04 Blood Supply Hub (Hub)
Cold Chain Dispatch & FEFO Pick
Once compatible units are verified, the Blood Supply Hub acts on the release command. Utilizes FEFO (First-Expire, First-Out) algorithms to auto-select and dispatch the optimal blood units under continuous IoT cold chain tracking.
