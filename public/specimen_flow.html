Specimen Flowchart

VN-BECS V2.0 System Integration

Document: Specimen Pipeline

Status: Approved

Ref: SOP 02 / SOP 08 / SOP 11

Step Subsystem / Feature Workflow & Verification Logic
01 Donor Center (LIMS)
Phlebotomy & Sample Extraction
When phlebotomy completes, bind the whole blood bag and sample tube using the ISBT-128 standard DIN bar code. Pre-dispatch is automatically registered and pushed to the core testing lab.
02 Immunohematology & Reference Lab (IRL)
Specimen Reception & Worklist Dispatch
Specimen arrives at the lab. The control tower lists the sample as "Pending Reception" (待接收檢體). Signing off automatically dispatches the tube into parallel workflows: serological IDM/NAT screening and crossmatch queues.
03 Analyzer Integration (LIS)
Automated Testing & LIS Sync
The specimen runs inside automated analytical platforms (e.g. cobas 6800, ARCHITECT). High-throughput assay results for HIV, HBV, HCV, NAT, and Serology are synced directly back to the BECS database.
04 Quality Assurance (QA) Node
Final Quality Release & Inventory Unlock
Once all IDM, NAT, and blood grouping checks are reactive-free and fully compatible, the system triggers the "Quality Released" status. This immediately unlocks the corresponding physical blood bags inside the Blood Supply Hub from TESTING to AVAILABLE status.
